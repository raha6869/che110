Soil Conservation

Protecting our most valuable resource

Why It Matters

  • Prevents soil erosion and degradation
  • Maintains essential biodiversity
  • Ensures long-term food security
  • Protects water quality and quantity
  • Reduces climate change impacts
  • Preserves ecosystem services

Take Action

  • Support local sustainable farmers
  • Practice composting at home
  • Reduce chemical fertilizer use
  • Plant native species in your garden
  • Minimize soil disturbance
  • Educate others about soil health
